Matter of
detail

You've probably heard the oft-repeated phrase “details matter”. Yes, it may be something you're tired of espousing, but it's true and present in everything. Details make the difference between a bland outfit and one that turns heads.

In most cases, it's something you just don't notice until you take a closer look. In others, it's extra something you just can't your finger on. All you need to know is that details definitely matters, and we're here to tell you how you can pay attention and try it for yourself.
